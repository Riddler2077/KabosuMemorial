    Kabosu Memorial ​

    The dog Kabosu, who became the hero of memes and the face of the Dogecoin cryptocurrency, died at the age of 18 from leukemia. This was announced by her owner Atsuko Sato on May 24, 2024.

    Who is Kabosu ​

    Kabosu is a Shiba Inu breed dog from Japan, known as the Doge meme dog. Kabosu became famous when the owner posted a photo with the dog on the Internet, and this picture was used for memes. It is difficult to say exactly when this happened. Some sources write about 2010, others about 2013.
